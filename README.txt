Project: Telegram Bot
Name: Flanders Cinema Bot

Description:
 The bot is designed to search for movies and cinemas. 
 In it you can see a list of upcoming cinemas and movies that 
 are currently broadcast there. 
 It is also possible to watch the movie online and add to favorites.


Platform: NodeJS + Express
Database: MongoBD

Additional libraries:

 Mongoose 
 https://mongoosejs.com/

 Geolib 
 https://www.npmjs.com/package/geolib

 LoDasg 
 https://lodash.com/


Functional:
 Get a list of movies
 Get a list of cinemas
 Add movie to favorites
 Get a link to watch online
 Identify the nearest movie theater
 Watch a list of movies that are broadcast in the cinema
